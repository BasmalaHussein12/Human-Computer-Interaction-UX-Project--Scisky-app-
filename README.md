# SciSky - Research Community App

## Overview
SciSky is a research-oriented community application designed for people interested in academic research across various fields.  
The platform allows users to share ideas, discuss papers, and engage in academic discussions through blogs and community interaction.

---

## Project Goal
The goal of this project was to design a digital space that supports researchers and students by:
- Facilitating knowledge sharing
- Encouraging discussion of research papers
- Creating a structured and accessible research community platform

---

## Research and Analysis
This project was developed after conducting a detailed analysis of existing applications in the market, studying their strengths and limitations to identify gaps in research communication platforms.

The design decisions were based on:
- Market analysis of similar research platforms
- User needs in academic and research communities
- Identified gaps in collaboration and knowledge sharing tools

---

## Key Features
- Blog system for sharing ideas and discussions  
- Paper sharing and exploration across different fields  
- Community-driven interaction for researchers and students  

---

## Tools Used
- Figma  
- UX Research and Market Analysis  

---

## Timeline
This project was developed over a period of two months, including research, analysis, and design iterations.

---

## Outcome
The result is a research-driven conceptual application that addresses gaps in existing platforms for academic collaboration and idea sharing.
